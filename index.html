Hi, I'm Giorgos Nikoulis
I was born and raised in Thessaloniki, the second biggest city in Greece. I completed my Doctoral degree in Computational Physics in 2022 and published six papers during my research. I acquired several skills in physics simulation and computer science, workin with C++ and Python, and web development with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Flask during my career.
